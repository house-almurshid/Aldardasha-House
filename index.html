About Aldardasha House

Established in January 2023, Aldardasha House is the heart of Dardasha Group Pvt Ltd, serving as its permanent office since March 27, 2025. It symbolizes unity, planning, and the future of our multi-venture group.

Our Space

Aldardasha House includes conference rooms, planning areas, and resource libraries for the Directors, Executive Council, and teams. It is a hub of innovation and collaboration.

Affiliated Cells

  • Educational Cell: Focused on future schooling ventures.
  • Healthcare Cell: Planning health centres for the community.
  • Industrial Cell: Building bakeries and food canteens to serve the public.
